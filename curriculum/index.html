Bio

Bianca Pereira is a Ph.D. candidate at Insight Centre at Federal University of Ireland, Galway. Previously she received her M.Sc. Degree in Informatics and Bachelor Degree in Computer Science from Federal University of Rio de Janeiro in 2011 and 2010 respectively. Her interests are: Semantic Web, Information Retrieval, Natural Language Processing and Ontology Modularization.
